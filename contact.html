Send us a message

Get in touch with TVL-RUG about passenger experience, service issues, meetings or general enquiries. We review all submissions and use feedback to support our work with operators and stakeholders across the Tees Valley.

Passenger feedback note:

TVL-RUG is not a train operator and cannot provide emergency travel assistance. For urgent disruption, refunds, lost property or immediate journey help, please contact the relevant operator or National Rail. For recurring issues, service patterns, station facilities or passenger experience matters, we welcome your feedback.
