Updated TVL-RUG logo to be unveiled

TVL-RUG will shortly unveil an updated logo as part of the group’s refreshed digital presence.

Featured update

Published: April 2026

TVL-RUG is preparing to unveil an updated group logo as part of wider work to refresh the group’s public website, improve communication, and create a stronger digital presence for passengers, members and stakeholders.

The updated identity will support the group’s work across the Tees Valley Line, helping provide a clearer and more consistent look across the website, social media, member communications and future publications.

This forms part of ongoing development work on the new TVL-RUG website, including improved public information pages, a dedicated service status area, route information, news updates and a future members area for documents and meeting resources.

What happens next?

Further announcements will follow once the updated logo is ready to be shared publicly.
