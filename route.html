The Tees Valley Line

A route connecting communities, workplaces, leisure destinations and key rail interchanges across the Tees Valley. Explore the line, its stations, and the role it plays in supporting local journeys.

Route overview

A line that links towns, coast and rail hubs

The Tees Valley Line serves a wide range of communities and destinations, linking local stations with major regional centres and onward national connections. It plays an important role for commuting, education, leisure and wider economic connectivity across the area.

At a glance

  • 13 stations across the core route
  • Key local and regional connections
  • Links communities, coast and rail hubs
  • Supports everyday and leisure journeys
