Meetings

TVL-RUG holds regular meetings to discuss passenger issues, rail developments, operator engagement and matters affecting communities across the Tees Valley Line.

TVL-RUG Monthly Meeting

First Tuesday of each month · 19:00 · Dinsdale station

Our monthly meeting gives members and supporters the opportunity to hear updates, discuss current rail issues, review local priorities and share views on the matters that affect passengers across the Tees Valley Line.

What to expect

Passenger and service updates

Discussion of service performance, local concerns, station issues and current developments affecting journeys across the line.

Stakeholder engagement

Opportunities to hear about current work, local priorities and wider rail matters affecting the Tees Valley Line and the communities it serves.

Regular arrangements

TVL-RUG meets on the first Tuesday of each month at 19:00 at Dinsdale station. Meetings are community-focused and provide a forum for discussion on services, passenger experience and wider rail matters affecting the line.

From time to time, meetings may also include guest speakers, special topics or updates from stakeholders and rail partners.

Meeting documents

Approved papers, minutes, newsletters and members-only updates will be available through the members area once login is enabled.

At a glance

  • First Tuesday of each month
  • 19:00 start
  • Dinsdale station

Who meetings are for

Meetings are intended to support the group’s work in representing passengers and engaging with communities, supporters and stakeholders interested in rail across the Tees Valley Line.
