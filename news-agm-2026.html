Northern presentation at TVL-RUG AGM

TVL-RUG’s 2026 Annual General Meeting will be followed by a presentation and Q&A with representatives from Northern.

AGM 2026

Published: April 2026

TVL-RUG’s 2026 Annual General Meeting will take place on 5 May 2026 at Lune Street Social Club in Saltburn.

Following the formal AGM business, the group will welcome Alex Jarvis and Marie Addison from Northern for a presentation and question-and-answer session.

This will provide an opportunity to hear more about Northern’s future ambitions for performance and passenger journeys, as well as giving members and attendees the chance to raise matters relevant to local services across the Tees Valley Line.

Event details

AGM: 5 May 2026 · Lune Street Social Club, Saltburn · Northern presentation and Q&A to follow the formal meeting.
