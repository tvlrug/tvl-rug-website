About TVL-RUG

The Tees Valley Line Rail User Group exists to represent passengers and support the continued promotion and improvement of rail services across the Tees Valley Line.

Who we are

TVL-RUG is a community-focused rail user group that brings together passengers, supporters and stakeholders with a shared interest in stronger rail services across the Tees Valley.

We aim to provide a constructive and informed voice for local rail users, encouraging better communication, stronger services and positive engagement between communities, operators and stakeholders.

Our purpose

TVL-RUG exists to support passengers, promote local rail travel and help ensure that the communities served by the line have a clear, credible and organised voice.

What we aim to do

Represent passengers

Bring forward passenger concerns, priorities and experience in a practical and constructive way.

Promote rail use

Encourage greater awareness, understanding and positive promotion of local rail services and stations.

Engage with stakeholders

Work with operators, elected representatives and community partners where it helps improve passenger outcomes.

Support improvement

Champion better journeys, stronger services and a more connected future for communities along the line.

At a glance

  • Passenger representation
  • Community rail advocacy
  • Constructive stakeholder engagement
  • Promotion of local rail services
