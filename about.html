About TVL-RUG

Learn more about the Tees Valley Line Rail User Group, what we stand for, and how we work to support rail passengers across the region.

Who we are

TVL-RUG exists to support and represent rail users across the Tees Valley Line. We provide a constructive voice for passengers and work to encourage better rail services, better information, and greater recognition of the role the railway plays in local life.

We engage with operators, stakeholders, and the wider community to help promote improvements, support rail use, and highlight the value of strong local services.

What we aim to do

Passenger representation

Bring forward passenger concerns and views in a constructive and organised way.

Promotion and advocacy

Encourage awareness and support for rail services, stations, and future opportunities.

Partnership working

Engage with operators, elected representatives, and stakeholders where it helps passengers.

Community focus

Support rail as part of wider community life, connectivity, and local opportunity.

At a glance

  • Passenger-focused
  • Community-based
  • Constructive engagement
  • Promotion of local rail services
