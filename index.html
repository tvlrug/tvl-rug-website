What we do

TVL-RUG exists to represent passengers, promote local rail services and support the long-term development of the railway across the communities served by the Tees Valley Line.

Represent passengers

We bring forward the views, concerns and priorities of rail users across the Tees Valley Line in a constructive and informed way.

Promote the railway

We support greater awareness and use of local rail services, stations and opportunities across the communities the line serves.

Engage constructively

We work positively with operators, local authorities and other stakeholders to support improvement and advocate for passengers.
