What we do

TVL-RUG works to represent rail users, encourage improvement, and support the long-term development of services across the Tees Valley Line.

Represent passengers

We gather views from rail users and raise recurring concerns and opportunities with the right bodies.

Promote the railway

We champion local rail travel and support positive awareness of stations, services, and future opportunities.

Engage constructively

We work with operators, public bodies, and stakeholders to help shape better outcomes for passengers.
